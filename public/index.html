Tutorial

Welcome to Tower Defense!

This tutorial will guide you through the basics of the game. Click 'Next' to continue or 'Skip' to exit the tutorial.

Game Objective

Your goal is to defend your base from waves of enemies by strategically placing towers along their path. If too many enemies reach the end, you lose!

Tower Loadout

Before starting a game, you can select up to 4 towers from your inventory to use in battle. Click the 'Loadout' button in the main menu to manage your towers.

Tower Gacha

You can obtain new towers by using the Tower Gacha. Spend silver to roll for different tower types with varying rarities.

Variant Gacha

Tower variants give special bonuses to your towers. Use the Variant Gacha to roll for different variants with varying rarities.

Placing Towers

During the game, click on a tower in the selection bar at the bottom, then click on an empty tile to place it. Towers cannot be placed on the enemy path.

Tower Types

Different towers have different strengths and weaknesses. Some can target flying enemies, others can target shadow enemies. Hover over a tower to see its stats.

Upgrading Towers

Click on a placed tower to upgrade it. Upgrades increase damage, range, or fire rate, but cost gold.

Waves

Click 'Start Wave' to begin sending enemies. Each wave gets progressively harder with more and stronger enemies.

Difficulty Levels

The game has several difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, Nightmare, Void, and special modes like Heaven and Hell. Higher difficulties give better rewards!

Currency

Silver is used for gacha rolls. Gems are premium currency for special rolls. Gold is in-game currency for placing and upgrading towers.

Ready to Play?

You're now ready to defend your base! Good luck!
